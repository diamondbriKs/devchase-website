Welcome to Devchase - LAH 2018

The Twilio based social experience


What even is this?

Devchase is a game hosted through Twilio, an API for sending SMS and MMS text messages. The game works on the concept of finding a person from an image sent to you, and exchanging a keyword to score a point. The players with the highest number of points are displayed on the leaderboard.(wip)

That sounds fun! How do I play?

Commands for reference:

  • Start - creates your account with your phone number
  • Queue - puts you into a queue to match with a partner
  • Clear - removes your account from database
